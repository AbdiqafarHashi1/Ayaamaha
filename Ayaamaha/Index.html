Quick Tips & Common Questions

Always double-check the specific photo requirements (size, background color, facial expression) for your target embassy before submission. Small errors in photos can lead to application delays! We can help ensure your photos meet all criteria.

For many visa types, an in-person appointment at the embassy or a designated visa application center in Nairobi is mandatory for biometrics (fingerprints and photo) and sometimes an interview. However, we handle all the preceding digital paperwork, form filling, and appointment booking to make the process as smooth as possible for you.

To register for a KRA PIN in Kenya, you'll typically need a copy of your National ID (for citizens), Alien ID (for resident foreigners), or Passport. For businesses, company registration documents are also required. We guide you through the exact requirements based on your specific situation.

Processing times vary greatly depending on the country, visa type, and time of year. Some can take a few weeks, others several months. We provide estimated timelines based on current embassy information and help you prepare well in advance. Our urgent processing add-on can also expedite our service portion.
